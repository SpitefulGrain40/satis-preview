My recommendation: Variant 1 or 4. Both make each active tab a miniature of the brand mark (glyph over a 60% hairline), which is a genuinely distinctive, ownable nav pattern — most apps just recolour the icon. V1 carries a touch more clarity (sage label too); V4 is more restrained and elegant. V2 is the safe "louder" option if either feels too quiet on real hardware. V5's pill works but muddies the hairline idea. Test V1 and V4 on your phone before locking.
